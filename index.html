Understanding GPA Calculation

GPA, or Grade Point Average, is a numerical representation of your academic performance. It's calculated by weighting your grades in each course by the number of credit hours for that course. A higher GPA generally indicates better academic achievement.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I add a course? Click the "Add Another Course" button to add more courses to your calculation.
  • What if I have a course with no credit hours? Enter 0 for credit hours.
  • Is this calculator accurate? This calculator provides estimates. Your school's official GPA calculation may differ slightly.
